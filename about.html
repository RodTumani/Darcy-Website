About Darcy

Darcy is redefining the U.S. Fashion Industry with its revolutionary circular fashion business model. Focused on sustainability, conscious consumption, and waste reduction, Darcy fosters a circular ecosystem that integrates eco-friendly materials, second-hand trading, and transparent production processes.

Led by our visionary CEO, Ms. Giovanna Tumani Soubhia Giesteira, Darcy aims to create a platform that transforms the way Americans view, purchase, and dispose of clothing. By encouraging sustainable fashion choices, Darcy is reducing textile waste, promoting environmental responsibility, and making a positive economic and social impact nationwide.
